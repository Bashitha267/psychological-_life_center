## Image Specifications

### Hero Images
- **Size:** 1920x600px (minimum)
- **Format:** JPG
- **Quality:** High (80-90%)
- **Content:** Serene nature scenes, therapy rooms, peaceful environments

### Service Icons/GIFs
- **Size:** 300x300px
- **Format:** GIF or MP4 (converted to GIF)
- **Loop:** Yes
- **Duration:** 2-5 seconds
- **Content:** Simple, clean animations representing each service

### Staff Photos
- **Size:** 600x600px (minimum)
- **Format:** JPG
- **Quality:** High (85-95%)
- **Style:** Professional headshots, neutral background
- **Lighting:** Even, professional

### Project Images
- **Thumbnails:** 800x600px
- **Gallery Images:** 1200x900px
- **Format:** JPG
- **Quality:** High (80-90%)
- **Content:** Community events, workshops, facilities

## Tips for Image Selection

1. **Maintain Consistency:** Use similar color tones and styles
2. **Quality First:** High-resolution images look more professional
3. **Optimize:** Compress images without losing quality
4. **Accessibility:** Include descriptive alt text (handled in PHP)
5. **Privacy:** Ensure proper consent for photos with identifiable people
